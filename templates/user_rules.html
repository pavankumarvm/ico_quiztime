Rules of the Quiz

  • All questions are Multiple Choice Questions with four options.
  • Once you click on ‘Take Quiz’ the quiz for that particular day will start.
  • You cannot attempt the same quiz again.
  • The quiz for one particular day is valid from 8.30 AM to 4.30 PM Every question has a time limit of 30 secs.
  • A live timer will appear on the upper right-hand corner.
  • Please click on the right answer from the four options before time runs out.
  • On clicking an answer, the next question will appear In case of no response, the next question will automatically appear after the time limit is over Each question has a certain number of points for the right answer which will be made known in the question itself.
  • This quiz does not have any negative points, so attempt all questions The faster you click on the correct answer, the more bonus points you will collect.
  • Your score for a particular quiz is the total of points scored for all questions inclusive of the bonus points Your final rank is based on the cumulative score earned by you across all the quizzes.
  • The Top 15 participants will qualify for the Finals, which will be held on-stage on the 14th of September.
  • Exact details will be communicated to you via email
